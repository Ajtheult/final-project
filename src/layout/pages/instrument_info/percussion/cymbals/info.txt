Type of instrument: Percussion

What is a cymbal

A cymbal is a common percussion instrument. Often used in pairs, cymbals consist
of thin, normally round plates of various alloys. The majority of cymbals are of 
indefinite pitch, although small disc-shaped cymbals based on ancient designs 
sound a definite note.
